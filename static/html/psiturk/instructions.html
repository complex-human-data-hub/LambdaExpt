Instructions

You will be asked to read several short documents and answer some comprehension questions about each.

After that, you will study a list of words. Each word will appear on the screen for two seconds.

Finally, you will see another list of words. For each one, decide whether you saw it in the earlier study list and press the matching key:

  • Y — yes, this word was in the study list
  • N — no, this word was not in the study list

The experiment finishes with a short demographics questionnaire and a debriefing.
